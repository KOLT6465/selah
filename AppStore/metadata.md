## Description

Selah brings a quiet moment of Scripture to your Mac menu bar.

Open Selah whenever you need a pause and find one thoughtfully selected Bible verse for the day. Today’s verse stays steady all day, then changes automatically with your local calendar after midnight.

BEAUTIFUL AND UNOBTRUSIVE

Selah lives in the menu bar, not the Dock. Its compact native interface is designed for comfortable reading in both light and dark appearances.

YOUR VERSES, CLOSE AT HAND

• Save verses you want to revisit
• Copy a verse and reference in one click
• Share through the standard macOS share menu
• Optionally open Selah when you log in

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Selah works entirely offline. There are no accounts, analytics, advertisements, trackers, subscriptions, or remote servers. Your saved verses and preferences remain on your Mac.

SCRIPTURE

Selah includes the public-domain World English Bible, Protestant Edition. Its daily collection is drawn from readable standalone verses throughout all 66 books.

Selah is free.
