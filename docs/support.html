How can we help?

Selah is intentionally simple, private, and offline. Most questions have a quick answer.

Where is Selah?

Selah lives in the macOS menu bar rather than the Dock. Look for the small book-and-sun icon near the top-right of your screen.

When does the verse change?

Selah chooses a different verse at the start of each day according to your Mac’s local calendar. The verse remains stable for the entire day.

Does Selah need internet access?

No. The World English Bible is included with the app, so daily verses and saved verses work entirely offline.

How do I stop Selah opening at login?

Open Selah, choose Settings, and turn off “Open Selah at login.” You can also manage login items in macOS System Settings.

How do I quit?

Open Settings and choose “Quit App.”

Still need help?

Email fivecapps@gmail.com. Please include your macOS version and a short description of what happened.
